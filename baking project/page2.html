Name

Soft Chocolate Chip Ice Cream Cookies

Prep: 15 minutes

Total Time: 50 minutes Serves:24 Rating: 4.5/5

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

    • Steps

    1. Preheat oven to gas mark 5. In a small bowl mix together flour, baking soda and salt.
    2. In a large bowl, beat softened butter and sugars with electric mixer on medium speed. Or mix with spoon for 1 minute until fluffy.
    3. Beat in egg and vanilla until smooth. Stir in flour mixture until blended then mix in chocolate chips.
    4. Onto an ungreased cookie sheet, drop dough by rounded tablespoonfuls 2 inches apart.
    5. Bake 8 to 10 minutes or until light brown (centers will be soft). Set aside to cool completely, about 30 minutes. Then add vanilla ice cream and sandwhich together or store in an airtight container until needed!
